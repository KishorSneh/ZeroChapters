    About Nothing to Read

    Nothing to Read is a visual, lightweight illustrated media discovery wall powered by AniList's live GraphQL API.

    Controls & Shortcuts

    • Hover or Click any cover to inspect full details and synopsis.
    • AL / MAL: Open the title directly on AniList or MyAnimeList.
    • ♥ Favorite: Save your favorite titles locally in your browser.
    • ↻ Shuffle: Randomize the wall layout anytime.
    • ☾ Theme: Toggle smooth dark & light modes.
